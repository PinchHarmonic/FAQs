IT Support FAQ

General Questions

How do I reset my password? ▼

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions to receive a reset link in your email.

Why is my internet connection slow? ▼

Slow internet can be caused by several factors including network congestion, outdated hardware, or signal interference. Try restarting your router or contacting IT support for further assistance.

Software Issues

How do I install the company VPN? ▼

Download the VPN client from the company intranet. Install the software and enter your login credentials provided by IT support to connect to the VPN.

What to do if my application crashes? ▼

If an application crashes, try restarting it. If the issue persists, check for software updates or contact IT support for further troubleshooting.
